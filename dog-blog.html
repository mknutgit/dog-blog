All About Me

Hi! I'm Sam! I love everything, including you for reading my amazing blog! I'm a St Bernard from the heart of the windy city, Chicago! I enjoy long walks through the park, fetching, and trying to murder the mailman. The love of my life is a fantastic stuffed bear named Patches.

This blog will chronicle my life. It is very interesting for a dog since I go up in the mountains and save peoples lives with the wiskey around my collar. When I am not sleeping, I like to sniff trees, grass and bushes.
